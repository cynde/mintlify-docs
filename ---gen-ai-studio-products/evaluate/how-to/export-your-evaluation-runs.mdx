# Export your Evaluation Runs

To download the results of your evaluation you can use the Export function. To export your runs, simply click on _Export Prompt Data._

![](https://docs.rungalileo.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1924900477-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F4jVWiQpRqmi04OnqZmn2%252Fuploads%252FE5FV9ZmsSR71nnPKuDxD%252FScreenshot%25202024-07-09%2520at%25208.31.54%25E2%2580%25AFAM.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D4189ab2e-1378-4496-a142-162700d483da&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=afd8da07&sv=1)

Your exported file will contain all Inputs, Outputs, Metrics, Annotations and Metadata for your Evaluation Run.

**Supported file types:**

*   CSV
    
*   JSONL
